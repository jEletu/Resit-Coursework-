Geography of Dundee

Key Geographic Features

River Tay

Dundee sits on the north bank of the River Tay, Scotland's longest river. The Tay Estuary provides a natural harbor.

Dundee Law

An extinct volcano offering panoramic views of the city and the North Sea. It rises 174 meters above sea level.

Climate

Temperature

Average highs of 16°C in summer and 6°C in winter.

Rainfall

Around 700mm annually, less than western Scotland.

Sunshine Hours

Dundee averages 1,400 hours of sunshine annually.
