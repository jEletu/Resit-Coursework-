History of Dundee

Historical Timeline

Prehistoric Times

Evidence of human settlement in the Dundee area dates back to the Mesolithic.

Medieval Period

Dundee was granted royal burgh status by King William the Lion in the late 12th century.

Industrial Revolution

Dundee became known for the three Js: jute, jam and journalism.

Modern Era

Today, Dundee is transforming into a hub for digital media and life sciences.
