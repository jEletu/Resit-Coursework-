About Dundee

Dundee is Scotland's fourth-largest city and is known for its history of scientific research, comics, and video games. It's situated on the north bank of the Firth of Tay on the east coast of Scotland.

The city has a population of about 150,000 people and is home to two universities, including the University of Dundee where you're studying!

Did you know?

Dundee is home to the Royal Research Ship Discovery, which was used for Antarctic expeditions.

Famous Exports

Dundee gave the world marmalade, the comic book character Desperate Dan, and the video game Grand Theft Auto.
